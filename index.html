I'm a Senior Android Developer based in Indonesia, with 4+ years of experience delivering scalable, production-ready applications. My primary focus is Kotlin Multiplatform (KMP) — writing shared business logic once and deploying native experiences across Android and iOS.

Away from the keyboard I explore Compose Multiplatform, contribute to architecture discussions, and stay close to the Android ecosystem.
