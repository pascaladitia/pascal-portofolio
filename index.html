01 / About

Crafting mobile experiences

I'm a Senior Android Developer based in Jakarta, Indonesia, with 4+ years delivering scalable, production-ready applications. My primary focus is Kotlin Multiplatform (KMP) — writing shared business logic once and deploying native experiences on both Android and iOS.

I build with Clean Architecture and MVVM, keeping codebases testable, maintainable, and easy to evolve. I've shipped across fintech, media, enterprise, and hospitality — from real-time WebSocket platforms to AI-powered OCR and banking systems.

When not coding, I explore Compose Multiplatform, fine-tune CI/CD pipelines, and stay close to the Android ecosystem.
